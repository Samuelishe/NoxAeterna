# Project Vision

Nox Aeterna is a professional desktop application for symbolic systems, focused first on astrology, Tarot, personal birth profiles, current transits, lunar events, and long-term symbolic history.

It should feel like:

- A digital esoteric cabinet.
- A personal occult archive.
- An astrological atlas.
- A symbolic observatory.
- An archive of time and interpretation.
- A tool for studying symbolic correspondences.

The application does not prove, convince, or debunk. It presents structured symbolic systems, historical correspondences, astronomical data, interpretive layers, and personal symbolic archives in a calm and serious form.

## Product Tone

The tone must be:

- Serious
- Calm
- Atmospheric
- Intelligent
- Restrained
- Premium
- Non-ironic

Nox Aeterna should respect symbolic traditions as cultural, historical, interpretive, and personal systems without claiming scientific proof for symbolic meaning.

## Non-Goals

Nox Aeterna is not:

- A joke generator.
- A TikTok witchcore app.
- A fake AI magic assistant.
- A meme occult app.
- A shallow daily horoscope app.
- A random esoteric quote machine.
- A product that invents unsourced symbolic systems without labeling them.

Avoid features framed as "daily cosmic energy score" or similar simplified engagement mechanics.

## Visual References

Useful references:

- European occultism.
- Victorian occult revival.
- Old astronomical atlases.
- Dark academia.
- Gothic archive aesthetics.
- Occult manuscripts.
- Astrolabes.
- Ephemerides.
- Brass, parchment, matte black, night sky.
- Cultist Simulator mood, without copying.
- Bloodborne-like atmosphere, without becoming game UI.

Avoid:

- Neon purple.
- Cheap glow effects.
- Cartoon magic.
- Overdecorated fantasy UI.
- AI mysticism aesthetics.
- Generic spiritual wellness design.

## Long-Term Direction

The long-term product should support:

- Personal profiles centered on birth data and symbolic history.
- Natal charts and planetary positions.
- Current transits and lunar events.
- Tarot readings with saved sessions and context.
- Structured interpretation based on symbolic layers.
- A personal archive of notes, readings, transits, and symbolic events.
- Exportable, precise chart renderings.
- Carefully curated visual assets.

The application should feel like a durable archive that grows with the user, not a one-time calculator.
